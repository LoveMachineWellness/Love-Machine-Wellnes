CNA Services (No Supervision Needed)

  • Check and record vital signs
  • Bathing, dressing, grooming, oral care
  • Toileting and incontinence care
  • Meals & feeding help
  • Mobility, transfers, walking, wheelchair help
  • Range of motion exercises
  • Change bed linens & clean room
  • Minor wound care only
  • Collect urine/stool for lab
  • Weigh and measure height
  • Apply compression stockings
  • Companionship, safety checks
  • Handwashing and PPE

CBRF Services (No Supervision Needed)

  • Medication reminders (no shots/narcotics)
  • Bathing, grooming, dressing help
  • Mobility and transfers
  • Toileting and incontinence
  • Meal prep and feeding
  • Light housekeeping, laundry
  • Social activities, companionship
  • Vital signs (if trained)
  • Monitor & document changes
  • Minor wound care only
  • Handwashing and PPE
  • Safety checks, fall prevention
  • Respite care for family

Medical Assistant Services (No Supervision Needed)

  • Take vital signs (BP, temp, pulse, O2, height, weight)
  • Prepare for exams, set up rooms
  • Collect health history
  • Minor wound care only
  • Vision/hearing screening
  • Suture/staple removal (if trained)
  • EKG (if trained)
  • Medication reconciliation
  • Patient education (handouts)
  • Clean rooms, stock supplies
  • Answer phones/messages
  • Give oral/topical meds (if allowed)

Phlebotomy Services (No Supervision Needed)

  • Blood draw (venipuncture, not IVs/arteries)
  • Fingerstick blood collection
  • Blood cultures (routine)
  • Glucose monitoring (fingerstick)
  • Collect urine/stool/swab for lab
  • Label & prep for lab
  • Bandage after blood draw, check comfort
